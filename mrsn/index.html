Description

Here, we provide numerical data of trajectories for nucleosynthesis calculations and the nucleosynthesis yields, based on magnetorotational supernova (MR-SN) models of NTT15 (Nishimura, Takiwaki and Thielemann 2015). You can freely download these data, and use for your nucleosynthesis and chemical evolution calculations.
